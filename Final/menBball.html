UCSD ALUMNI ATHLETES

The network of UCSD alumni athletes continues to expand and former Tritons are doing new and exciting things around the world. On an ongoing basis, we will keep you informed as to the whereabouts and activities of the men and women who once wore the blue and gold.

Purpose

  • To Provide opportunities for our former student athletes to reconnect with each other and with UCSD.
  • To Support and interact with each other through social activities and networking opportunities.
  • To Support the University, Athletic Department and particular sports through events and projects in conjunction with sport staffs.
  • To Promote Triton Athletics in the community.

Benefits

  • Invitations to social events for your specific sport
  • Complimentary ticket to any home regular season game
  • Alumni Association benefits such as discounts on certain merchandize in the SDSU bookstore, University publications, admission to homecoming events, and assorted other discounts around San Diego.
